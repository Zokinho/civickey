## App Store Description (English)

### Short Description (for Google Play - 80 chars max)
Stay connected to your community with collection schedules, events & alerts.

### Full Description

**Never miss a collection day again.**

CivicKey puts your city's essential services right in your pocket. Get personalized collection schedules, local events, important announcements, and road closure alerts — all tailored to your neighborhood.

**COLLECTION REMINDERS**
- View your complete waste collection schedule
- Get timely reminders the evening before pickup
- Customize reminder times to fit your routine
- Support for garbage, recycling, compost, and more

**COMMUNITY EVENTS**
- Discover local workshops, festivals, and activities
- Browse upcoming events in your municipality
- Never miss what's happening in your neighborhood

**INSTANT ALERTS**
- Receive important announcements from your city
- Stay informed about schedule changes and emergencies
- Get notified about road closures and construction

**DESIGNED FOR EVERYONE**
- Clean, intuitive interface
- Full bilingual support (English & French)
- Works offline with cached schedules
- No account required — just select your zone and go

**PRIVACY FIRST**
- No personal data collected
- No tracking or analytics
- All preferences stored locally on your device

CivicKey is the simple, privacy-focused way to stay connected to your community.

## App Privacy Details (for App Store Connect)

### Data Not Collected
CivicKey does not collect any data from users.

### Data Linked to You
None

### Data Used to Track You
None

### Privacy Practices
- All user preferences (zone selection, language, notification time) are stored locally on the device
- No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted
- Municipal data (schedules, events, announcements) is fetched from Firebase but requires no user identification
- No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are used
